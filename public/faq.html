Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, payment methods, and product care at আভরণী.

How long does delivery take inside Bangladesh?
Inside Dhaka, orders are delivered within 24 to 48 hours. For orders outside Dhaka, delivery takes 2 to 4 business days via courier services (Steadfast / RedX / SA Paribahan).
What are the delivery charges?
Standard delivery inside Dhaka is ৳60. Outside Dhaka delivery charge is ৳120. Special express delivery options are available upon request.
Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Bank Transfers. You can select your preferred payment option at checkout.
Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available everywhere?
Yes! We provide Cash on Delivery all across Bangladesh so you can inspect your parcel before handing over payment to the delivery agent.
What is your return & replacement policy?
If you receive a defective, damaged, or wrong item, you can request an instant return or exchange within 7 days of delivery. Please visit our Return Product page to submit a request.
Can I change my dress size after ordering?
Yes, if the desired size is in stock, we will exchange it for you free of charge (customer pays the delivery fee for size swaps).
Are the product images 100% real?
Yes! All product images displayed on आभाৰણી are 100% original high-resolution studio photos of our actual stock. What you see is exactly what you receive.
How should I care for handcrafted jewelry & ornaments?
Keep your ornaments dry and away from direct perfume, hairspray, or harsh chemicals. Store each piece in a zip-lock pouch or velvet box to maintain shine and prevent tarnishing.
